----------------------------------------------> [Base Script] <----------------------------------------------

	--------------------------------> Trigonometry and Angular Maths <--------------------------------

	cossin
		Arguments:
			1) angle
		Returns:
			(real[2]) [cos(angle), sin(angle)]
		Description:
			Reverse of sincos().
			cossin(n) is equivalent to sincos(-n + 90).

	rcossin
		Arguments:
			1) angle (radians)
		Returns:
			(real[2]) result
		Description:
			cossin() for radians input.
			rcossin(n) is equivalent to rsincos(-n + pi / 2).
